CliniQ Privacy Policy

How we collect, handle, and protect your data

1. Introduction

CliniQ (“we”, “our”, “the application”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your personal information when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of data:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, feature interactions, device type.
  • Health Inputs: Self-entered medical notes or symptom checks.
  • Location (Optional): Only when using emergency or map features.

3. How Your Information Is Used

  • To provide core functionality of the CliniQ platform.
  • To offer health insights and emergency assistance features.
  • To improve app performance and user experience.
  • To communicate important service updates.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your information is stored securely and protected using industry-standard encryption, role-based access control, and safe data handling practices. No health data is shared without explicit consent.

5. Third-Party Sharing

CliniQ does not sell your personal data. Limited sharing may occur when:

  • Integrating with emergency providers (only with user-triggered action).
  • Using analytics tools to improve service performance.

6. Your Rights

  • Request access to data we hold about you.
  • Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Opt out of non-essential data collection.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised “Last Updated” date.
